Stuffed Eggplant

Description

This delicious stuffed eggplant with a cheesy top is a must-try recipe! Great as a hearty main but you can buy the mini eggplants and use them for a side dish or appetizer.

Ingredients

  • eggplant
  • Olive oil
  • Seasonings
  • Sausage
  • Vegetables
  • Pasta sauce
  • Bread crumbs
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. scoop out the flesh, brush the shell with oil, and season.
  2. Chop the reserved eggplant flesh into bite-sized pieces
  3. Cook sausage, vegetables, and garlic in the remaining oil
  4. Stir in reserved eggplant, basil, and sauce, mix in some of the panko and cheese
  5. stuff the shell and sprinkle the remaining cheese and panko over the top
  6. Bake untill the eggplant is tender
